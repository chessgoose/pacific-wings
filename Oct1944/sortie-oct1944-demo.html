October 1944 Sorties

This is full-month playback of the 12th Bombardment Group of the U.S. Army Air Force built from a dataset of sortie reports from the National Archives. The current archive-backed run includes 67 plotted missions across October 1944, where the 12th Bombardment Group was stationed in India and fighting against Japanese forces in Burma.

National Archives visit, College Park, Maryland

The October 1944 sortie dataset in this project is based on photographs taken during an in-person visit to the National Archives at College Park, Maryland. The archival catalog anchor for the material is the following National Archives record.

catalog.archives.gov/id/596339
