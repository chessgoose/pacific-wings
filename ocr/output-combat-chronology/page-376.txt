5/5/44 Seventh AF

During 4/5 May B-24’s from Kwajalein stage through Eniwetok and bomb Truk. During the day B-25’s
from Eniwetok strike Ponape, and 10 from Makin hit Jaluit and Wotje, using Majuro as rearming base
between the attacks.

Sottevast and La Glacerie, fail to bomb because of total overcast at tgts.

5/6/44 Ninth AF

24 A-31’s continue pounding positions near Buthidaung. 14 B-25’s bomb Indainggyi, destroy Sibong

Mogaung Valley. 18 FBs and 6 B-25’s spt ground forces at Mawlu while 3 B-25’s and 4 P-51’s hit posi-

5/6/44 Fourteenth AF

61 P-40’s and 5 B-25’s attack tgts throughout S China and Indochina. Numerous vehicles and troops

sunk at Campha Port and numerous sampans and small craft sunk or damaged at Cat Ba |. Main
