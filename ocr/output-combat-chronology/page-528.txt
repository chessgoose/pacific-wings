10/28/44 Ninth AF

HQ 9th Bomb Div transfers to Reims. 45 9th Bomb Div aircraft bomb rail bridges at Sinzig,
Kempenich, and Ahrweiler, and A/F at Euskirchen. Ftrs escort bmrs, fly sweeps and armed rcn over W
Germany, attack 6 bridges and 1 tunnel, and spt US XIX Corps in Belgium near German border.

10/28/44 Twelfth AF
Bad weather again grounds MBs, and restricts XXII TAC. FBs and firs, flying 65 sorties, attack vehi-
cles and trains in Turin-Milan-Genoa area.

10/28/44 Fifteenth AF
Bad weather again limits operations. 10 B-17’s bomb Klagenfurt aircraft factory. 6 P-38’s fly rcn mis-
sions, during early part of the night. 8 B-17’s bomb Munich W M/Y. 1 bombs Erlsbach.

10/28/44 Tenth AF
80-plus FBs pound numerous tgts including town areas, troops, railroad facilities, and a variety of
T/Os at Mannaun, Manoi, Sinkan, Winwa, Man Mao, Myazedi, Pinwe, Naba, Yebawgyi, and Kangon.

10/28/44 Fourteenth AF
Ftrs strafe villages, troops, and horses in Menghsu-Konghow area, pound bridges around
Kaotienhsu, and hit Yangtong A/F and shipping at Hongay and Wuchou.

10/28/44 FEAF
B-24’s, fighting bad weather, bomb A/F at Puerto Princesa. During 28/29 Oct, B-24’s bomb
Wilhelmina Docks area. B-25’s and P-38’s blast town of Amboina.

10/28/44 Twentieth AF
Marianas-based XXI BC flies its first combat mission when 14 B-29’s attack sub pens on Dublon |. 4
others (1 carrying Gen Hansell, CG XXI BC) abort. About a third of the bombs fall in general tgt area.
