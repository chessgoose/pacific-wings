11/23/44 Tenth AF

More than 50 P-47’s spt ground forces in Pinwe and Bhamo areas. 16 sweep and strafe A/Fs and
many T/Os from Anisakan to Nawnghkio. 3 hit ammo dump at Man Naung. 21 P-47’s sweep roads in
Ye-U area and strafe rail installations at Kanbalu. 10 B-25’s knock out Tantabin main bridge and
Tangon and Thegyaung bypass bridges, and blast approaches to Tangon main bridge and Tantabin
bypass bridge. 282 transport sorties are flown to forward bases and frontline areas.

11/23/44 Fourteenth AF

12 B-25’s bomb storage area near Lashio and 8 hit Kutkai and Wanling. 2 B-24’s bomb Kowloon
Docks. More than 120 P-40’s, P-51’s and P-38’s hit T/Os throughout SW and SE China. 32 of the FBs
spt ground forces in Chefang area.

11/23/44 FEAF
B-24’s bomb Matina A/F.

11/23/44 Seventh AF
17 B-24’s from Guam hit shipping at Chichi Jima and Haha Jima and bomb town of Okimura.

11/24/44 Ninth AF
Weather cancels all operations except for 3 XIX TAC ftrs which fly night intruder mission in
Saarbrucken, Zweibrucken, and Homburg/Saar areas.

11/24/44 Twelfth AF
B-25’s, in spt of British Eighth Army, bomb defenses in Faenza area. Bad weather restricts FBs to 2
missions which damage a road bridge and cut rail line S of Modena.

11/24/44 Fifteenth AF
P-38’s and P-51’s fly photo and weather rcn missions.

11/24/44 Tenth AF

32 P-47’s fly close spt strikes in Pinwe and Bhamo sectors. 50 P-47’s attack troops and supply areas
at Panma, Hpa-Hpen, Nawng-Sang, Kawlin, Wahkyet, Kawng ai, and in Pintha area, 8 damage
bridges at Meza, Namhkai, and Hsenwi while 10 others hit Lashio A/F. 6 B-25’s pound storage and
ferry area at Meza and 4 damage approaches to Namhkai and Hsenwi road bridges. 323 transport
sorties are flown to forward areas.

11/24/44 Fourteenth AF

21 B-24’s bomb warehouse area and docks at Hankow. 3 others hit Haiphong area. 22 B-25’s bomb
storage area near Lashio, T/Os in Hankow area, and towns of Wanling, Siangtan, Wuchang, and Wan
Lai-Kam. 120-plus P-40’s, P-38’s and P-51’s on armed rcn attack many T/Os in E Burma and SW and
SE China, concentrating on river and rail trafflc and supplies at Chefang, Heng- shan, and Sinshih-
Changsha area.

11/24/44 FEAF

B-24’s bomb AA positions and T/Os at Camp Downes and Panalisan Pt and US ftrs attack and
destroy several airplanes over Carigara Bay and Leyte area. B-24’s hit AA positions and other tgts at
Matina A/F.
