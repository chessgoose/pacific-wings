8/10/44 FEAF

B-24’s attack Yap, concentrating on AA positions, A/Fs and town of Yap. Others hit A/Fs at Galela,
Lolobata and Namlea. B-25’s bomb Langgoer A/F. FBs attack Sorong, Manokwari, and villages along
W coast of Geelvink Bay. FBs continue to pound forces between Aitape and Wewak. Large-scale TC
missions are flown to forward bases, especially in Schouten Is.

8/10/44 Twentieth AF

2 missions are flown during 10/11 Aug. In one, 24 B-29’s, out of Chengtu, bomb urban area of
Nagasaki and 3 others hit T/Os. The B-29’s claim 1 ftr shot down, the first such claim (except proba-
bles) by the B-29’s. In other missions, the first staged through China Bay, 31 B-29’s bomb oil refiner-
ies at Palembang, 8 mine the Moesi R nearby, and 3 hit T/Os and a secondary tgt. The first attack,
from Ceylon to Sumatra, is the longest single-stage combat flight (about 3,900 mi) by B-29’s during
the war.

8/10/44 Seventh AF

B-24’s, flying their first mission from Saipan, pound Iwo Jima, beginning the AAF’s neutralization cam-
paign of that island. Saipan-based P-47’s hit Tinian and Pagan Is. All organized resistance on Guam
ends. B-24’s from Kwajalein hit Wotje.

8/10/44 Eleventh AF
4 B-25’s on a shipping sweep spot 2 patrol boats 75 mi ESE of Shimushu |. One is sunk, the other is
damaged.

8/11/44 Eighth AF

More than 850 HBs attack 13 M/Ys, fuel dumps, A/Fs, and T/Os, in NE France and Paris area, and 23
arsenal areas, barracks, concrete emplacements and heavy arty posts in and around Brest, escorted
by 8 ftr gos and a sq. 5 gps later strafe ground tgts.

8/11/44 Ninth AF

LBs and MBs from 97th, 98th, and 99th Combat Bomb Wgs attack bridges at Montrichard, Oissel,
Fismes, and Creil/Saint-Maximin, gun defenses at lle de Cezembre and Saint-Malo, and ammo dump
at Foret de Roumare. Ftrs cover assault area, escort IX BC, and fly armed rcn in battle area and
extensively over N France.

8/11/44 Twelfth AF
B-25’s, B-26’s, and P-47’s strike gun positions along French and Italian coasts W of Genoa as DRA-
GOON assault force begins movement from Naples area toward tgts in S France.

8/11/44 Tenth AF
5 B-25’s bomb encampment area and underground shelters at Kadu. 4 others abort because of bad
weather. 6 P-40’s attack Japanese-occupied temple at Shwekyina and Japanese HQ at Bhamo.

8/11/44 Fourteenth AF

23 B-24’s bomb Changsha, 16 B-25’s pound Hengyang over 40 P-51’s and P-40’s bomb bridges, vil-
lages, warehouses, trucks, troops, and other T/Os in Hengyang area. 26 P-40’s attack T/Os at or near
Chuting, Puchou, and Yungfengshih.
