1/30/43 Eleventh AF

2 patrols, each composed of 1 B-25 and 4 P-38’s, fly over Amchitka and are recalled early due to
weather. 1 B-17, upon an alleged submarine sighting, drops 4 depth charges and 1 bomb whereupon
a whale breaks water. Weather cancels other missions.
