11/25/44 Seventh AF
7 Guam-based B-24’s, escorting photo aircraft over Bonin and Kazan Is, bomb Chichi Jima, Muko
Jima, and Haha Jima.

11/25/44 Eleventh AF
A B-24 aborts an armed photo mission over Matsuwa due to weather and instead radarbombs Kurabu
A/F. B-25’s cancel a shipping sweep due to weather.

11/26/44 Eighth AF

1,000-plus B-17’s and B-24’s bomb rail viaducts at Altenbeken and Bielefeld, Misburg oil refinery,
M/Ys at Hamm, Osnabruck, Hannover, Gutersloh, Bielefeld, and Herford, and 7 T/Os. 15 ftr gps fly
escort. About 550 ftrs are encountered. HB losses total over 35 (25 to ftrs). US ftrs claim over 100 air
victories.

11/26/44 Ninth AF

173 MBs and LBs bomb supply, storage, stores, and ordnance depots at Gaulsheim, Bergzabern,
Giessen, Reichenbach, and Homburg/Saar. Ftrs fly sweeps and armed rcn over W Germany, search
for lost A-20, escort 9th Bomb Div, and spt US 29th Inf Div at Bourheim and XX and XII Corps in area
of the Maginot Line and German-French border.

11/26/44 Twelfth AF

Slight improvement in weather permits FBs to increase operations. P-47’s closely spt ground forces in
US Fifth Army battle area in the Apennines S of Bologna and cut rail lines in over 30 places N of the
immediate battle zone.

11/26/44 Fifteenth AF
39 P-38’s fly offensive sweep over Seregelyes A/F and strafe nearby road and rail traffic. Other ftrs fly
rcn and escort.

11/26/44 Tenth AF

16 P-47’s continue close spt strikes. About 60 FBs hit concentrations, supply and ammo dumps, and
rail tgts at Panghkai, Kunmong, Mabein, Lashio, Panku, and around Meza. 7 others on railroad sweep
hit T/Os between Maymyo and Man Pyen. Transports fly more than 300 sorties to forward areas.

11/26/44 Fourteenth AF

19 B-25’s and 20 P-51’s blast railway cars, station, and track, hit several trucks, and hit town areas at
Hochih and Phu Lang Thuong 6 B-25’s damage bridge at Kengluang. 90-plus P-40’s, P-51’s, and P-
38’s hit river, rail, and road traffic and other T/Os over wide S China areas, 40 of them concentrating
on tgts between Kweiyi and Changsha and around Liuchow.

11/26/44 FEAF

40-plus B-24’s, some with ftr spt, bomb La Carlota and Talisay A/Fs. FBs hit A/F and various T/Os
throughout the area. B-25’s pound A/Fs in Amboina-Ceram area and B-24’s bomb A/F near Davao.
Others bmrs and ftrs fly light strikes and armed rcn missions against a variety of tgts in N Celebes, N
Borneo, S Luzon, Mindanao, and Halmahera.

11/26/44 Seventh AF
P-47’s from Saipan strafe A/F on Pagan. During the night a B-24 on snooper mission from Guam
bombs lwo Jima. B-24’s from Angaur bomb Arakabesan.
