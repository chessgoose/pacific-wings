12/24/44 Tenth AF

38 P-47’s hit troop concentrations and supply areas at Pangkai, Man-namman, Man Om, Mong Nge,
and Kyanhnyat. 6 spt ground forces in Si-U sector while 12 hit T/Os along Sinlum-Nawghkio rail line. 8
P-47’s strafe A/F at Lashio. 287 transport sorties are flown to forward areas.

12/24/44 Fourteenth AF

6 B-25’s blast Kunlong ferry area 3 B-24’s claim 1 tanker sunk in S China Sea. 100-plus P-40’s, P-
51’s, and P-38’s on armed rcn attack T/Os throughout S China, especially river, road, and rail traffic,
troops, and buildings around Hengyang, Lingling, Siangtan, and Changsha. Also Hong Kong area
shipping is pounded (1 tanker claimed sunk and other ships damaged) and 30-plus enemy airplanes
claimed destroyed at Tsinan A/F.

12/24/44 FEAF

B-24’s bomb Clark Field, Puerto Princesa, and Kudat A/Fs. B-25’s and FBs blast Goeroea area, bomb
Silay A/F and hit San Roque barracks, Zamboanga waterfront, and Davao Bay shipping. Again FEAF
aircraft on miscellaneous small operations attack many tgts throughout NEI and Phil Is.

12/24/44 Twentieth AF
23 XXI BC B-29’s attack 2 A/Fs on Iwo Jima.

12/24/44 Seventh AF

12 B-24’s from Saipan and 38 from Guam strike lwo Jima. 17 Saipan-based P-38’s make low-level
strafing attack on Iwo Jima. 23 other B-24’s, based on Saipan, bomb Chichi Jima. 2 B-24’s on harass-
ment strikes from the Marianas hit lwo Jima during 24/25 Dec.

12/25/44 Eighth AF
Over 350 HBs hit 18 tgts (mostly rail bridges and comm centers) in the tac area W of the Rhine. 9
escorting ftr gos encounter over 300 ftrs and claim more than 40 kills.

12/25/44 Ninth AF

Nearly 650 MBs and LBs hit rail and road bridges, comm centers and T/Os in W Germany and break-
through area. Ftrs, including an Eighth AF gp loaned to Ninth, escort 9th Bomb Div, fly patrols and
armed rcn, and spt US III, VIII, and XII Corps along S battleline of enemy salient from Echternach to
NW of Neufchateau.

12/25/44 Twelfth AF
Clearing weather in Po Valley permits FBs to successfully cut numerous rail lines running N to
Brenner Pass, and lines in E Po Valley, destroying or damaging several locomotives.

12/25/44 Fifteenth AF

253 HBs hit Brux synthetic oil plant and main M/Y at Wels. 145 others bomb M/Ys at Plattling, Villach,
Hall, Graz, Innsbruck (2), and Rosswein and Innsbruck AA position and rail siding. Over 30 ftrs escort
Brux-WelsRosswein-Plattling raids. Other P-38’s and P-51’s fly ren escort and cover MATAF B-25’s on
Yugoslav supply run.

12/25/44 Tenth AF

4 P-47’s bomb and strafe troop concentrations and supplies at Mabein. 8 others sweep Burma Road
stretches and strafe Lashio A/F. Transports fly 259 sorties delivering men and supplies to forward
bases and frontline areas.
