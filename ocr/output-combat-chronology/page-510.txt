10/5/44 Fifteenth AF
Bad weather causes recall of HBs.

10/5/44 Tenth AF
11 P-47’s hit Mawlu and attack locomotives and T/Os in Naba area. 8 B-25’s attack bridges at
Namhkai, damaging the main bridge. TCs fly over 250 sorties to various locations in CBI.

10/5/44 Fourteenth AF

12 B-25’s and 22 FBs attack Samshui, Koyiu and Takhing. 8 hit T/Os in Canton-Wuchou area, and 3
bomb storage area at Mangshih. 50-plus P-40’s and P-51’s over wide areas of S China attack river-
craft, road trafflc, bridges, town areas, and troops.

10/5/44 FEAF

B-24’s bomb A/Fs in Kendari area. B-25’s and P-38’s hit Kairatoe A/F, town of Amboina and numerous
coastal and shipping T/Os in Amboina-Ceram area. A-20’s, B-25’s’s, and FBs attack Urarom, Simora
Pt area, Doom |, Babo, Efman and Samate A/Fs and disposal areas. A-20’s blast Japanese bivouacs
and supply areas near Sarmi.

10/5/44 Seventh AF

Saipan-based P-47’s hit Pagan with rockets and bombs. A B-24 bombs gun positions on the islands.
B-25’s from the Gilberts bomb runways and gun positions on Nauru while B-24’s from the Marshalls
bomb an A/F at Moen and during 5 /6 Oct strike Wake.

10/5/44 Eleventh AF
At dawn 2 B-24’s bomb Kashiwabara and Kurabu Cape.

10/6/44 Eighth AF

Nearly 1,200 HBs attack 11 primaries, including oil plants, aircraft factories, ordnance facilities, and AF
trg school, in N Germany, and 19 T/Os including A/Fs at Stralsund and Stade. 16 ftr gps in escort
claim 19 air victories.

10/6/44 Ninth AF

300-plus MBs and LBs hit M/Ys, barracks, and ammo dump at Hengelo and Duren and bridges at
Arnhem and Aldenhoven. Ftrs fly armed rcn in Dusseldorf, Aachen, Trier, Dieuze, and Koblenz areas,
sweeps and patrols in forward areas, attack railroads in Dorsel area, and spt US First, Third, and
Seventh Armies’ forces in E France and W Germany.

10/6/44 Twelfth AF
Weather cancels all operations of MB wgs and XII FC.

10/6/44 Fifteenth AF
Weather cancels bombing operations. 35 P-38’s strafe A/Fs at Sedhes, Megalo Mikra, Megara,
Eleusis, and Tatoi A/Fs, and 55 P-51’s strafe Kalamaki A/F.

10/6/44 Tenth AF

20-plus P-47’s bomb troop concentrations in Mawlu and Bilumyo and damage approach to bridge at
Seywa. 8 B-25’s attack bridges at Hsenwi, damaging main bridge but causing little harm to bypass
bridges. Transport operations continue on large scale in CBI.
