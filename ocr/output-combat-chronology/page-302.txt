are at Rosieres-en-Santerre, Amiens/Glisy, Cormeilles-en-Vexin, and Grandvilliers.

2/6/44 Twelfth AF

B-26’s bomb Orte M/Y while B-25’s hit Frascati road junction. A-20’s bomb Campoleone railroad sta-
tion and town of Roccasecca. P-40’s bomb Cisterna di Latina, Santa Lucia (near Campoleone), Cori
(and nearby road junction), and Atina. A-36’s hit San Stefano al Mare, Cisterna di Latina, Frascati and
Albano Laziale, and strike numerous vehicles, railroad cars and other T/Os on armed rcn missions N
and S of Rome. German counteroffensive against Anzio beachhead and on Cassino front forces Allied
withdrawal.

B-25’s and B-24’s pound Bunabun Harbor, Madang, and tgts from Bogia to Cape Croisilles. Others
