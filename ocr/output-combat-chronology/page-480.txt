Several P-47’s bomb and strafe town of Man Sai.

8/30/44 Fourteenth AF

B-25’s attack Hengyang, Pailochi, and Hankow A/Fs, roads in Nanyo and Changsha areas, and boats
between Changsha and Hengyang, and Kichun and Wuhsueh. In Kweiyi and Sintsiang areas 33 P-
40’s claim 58 trucks destroyed, 175 damaged, and at least 100 Japanese killed. 10 P-51’s hit scat-
tered T/O’s in same areas. 21 P-40’s hit barracks, trucks, and a bridge in Siangsiang and Siangtan
region. 34 P-40’s and P-51’s attack a variety of tgts, including railroad traffic and facilities, occupied
areas, and trucks, at Yangtien, between Hengshan and Nanyo, NE of Ichang, SW Hengshan, and
near Hengyang.

8/30/44 FEAF

Koror and Malakal are pounded by B-24’s. B-24’s hit Wasile Bay storage and personnel areas while
B-25’s make low-level attack on Kaoe town. P-38’s bomb oil tanks, barracks, and AA positions at
Boela. P-47’s hit Urarom runway and Manokwari storage area, P-38’s bomb A/Fs at Babo and
Ransiki, and P-39’s hit T/Os along W coast of Geelvink Bay.

8/30/44 Seventh AF
A B-24 on armed rcn from Saipan bombs Yap. P-47’s strafe positions and storage areas on Pagan.
Kwajalein-based B-24’s hit Mille.

8/31/44 Ninth AF

99 B-26’s and A-20’s bomb ammo dump at Foret d’Arques and gun positions at lle de Cezembre. Ftrs
fly armed rcn in Amiens, Saint-Quentin, Albert, and Arras areas, ground forces cover for 3 armd divs,
battleship cover, and also dive-bomb Ile de Cezembre.

8/31/44 Twelfth AF

MBs attack railroad bridges in Po Valley, cutting the bridge at Mira. A-20’s attack T/Os in Po Valley
during 30/31 Aug and along with FBs hit comm N of Arno R. Other FBs attack comm tgts in France as
US Seventh Army pushes toward Lyon.

8/31/44 Fifteenth AF

45 P-51’s strafe A/F at Reghin, while 97 others strafe A/Fs at Oradea and Kecskemet. Ftrs claim a
record number of over 150 aircraft destroyed on the ground. Starting Operation REUNION, 36 B-17’s
evacuate US airmen interned in Rumania from Bucharest (which falls to the Soviet Army today) to
Kari.

8/31/44 Tenth AF
6 B-25’s bomb T/Os at Katha and 3 hit bridges at Bawgyo and Hsenwi.

8/31/44 Fourteenth AF

12 B-24’s bomb Takao harbor, damaging dock area and claiming 2 tankers sunk. 14 B-25’s attack Tien
Ho, White Cloud, Kai Tek, and Hengyang A/Fs. 8 B-25’s attack numerous trucks S of Sint-siang and
near Sinshih, hit roads S of Nanyo and damage a freighter near Sinshih. 60-plus Fbs attack trucks,
barracks, supplies, rivercraft, bridges and troops in or near Sinshih, Changsha, Yangtien, Hengyang,
Nanyo, Siangtan, Teian, and Shihhweiyao.

8/31/44 Seventh AF
Saipan-based P-47’s strafe gun positions at A/F on Pagan. Yap and Pagan are bombed by single B-
24's.
