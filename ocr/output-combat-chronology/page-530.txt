10/30/44 Fifteenth AF
During 30/31 Oct 3 B-24’s bomb Klagenfurt M/Y, P-38’s fly rcn missions, while bad weather cancels
all other operations.

10/30/44 Tenth AF

10 B-25’s knock out bridges at Namhkai, Wuntho, Thegyaung, and Nankan and damage others at
Okkyin and Zawchaung. 50-plus P-47’s knock out Hpao Nam R bridge and strafe T/Os at several
locations; hit several bridges throughout N Burma rail corridor, damaging or knocking out each tgt; spt
ground forces at Naba Station and Pinwe; and hit troop concentrations at Mansi and Man yut.

10/30/44 Fourteenth AF
13 B-24’s lay mines in Victoria Harbor. P-40’s bomb installations around Phu Lang Thuong and hit
junks at sea.

10/30/44 FEAF

B-24’s pound Bacolod A/F, while B-25’s and FBs hit San Roque A/F and barges at Zamboanga. P-
38’s hit Sandakan A/F. B-24’s bomb Makassar wharf area while P-40’s, over NE peninsula, hit various
T/Os. P-38’s hit Piroe. Urarom A/F is again bombed by A-20’s and B-25’s.

10/30/44 Twentieth AF
8 B-29’s from the Marianas bomb sub pens on Dublon |, 9 others bomb 2 mi beyond tat.

10/30/44 Seventh AF
8 B-24’s on armed rcn missions from Guam bomb Yap. 1 B-24 from Saipan, during 30/31 Oct snoop-
er mission, bombs lwo Jima. Saipan-based P-47’s hit Pagan. B-25’s from Makin strike Nauru.

10/30/44 Eleventh AF
3 B-24’s fly armed rcn over Matsuwa and Onnekotan.

10/31/44 Ninth AF
Weather forbids bmr operations and limits ftrs. XIl and XIX TACs fly patrols, sweeps, and armed rcn
over E France and W Germany. XII TAC also supports US Seventh Army elements in Metz area.

10/31/44 Twelfth AF

B-26’s hit bridge and causeway at Nervesa della Battaglia and bridges at Montebello and Piazzola sul
Brenta. FBs attack guns and positions in battle area S of Bologna in the Apennines, and comm and
shipping tgts in Po Valley and on Po R.

10/31/44 Fifteenth AF
P-38’s fly photo and weather rcn. 174 B-24’s, dispatched against tgt in Yugoslavia, are forced to
return because of weather.

10/31/44 Tenth AF

60-plus P-47’s attack occupied areas and supply areas at Namun, Bhamo, and Nakang, and railroad
bridges, locomotive shelters, and rolling stock along the KyaikthinNaba line. 2 B-25’s attack T/Os from
Katha to Bhamo along the Irrawaddy R.

10/31/44 Fourteenth AF
6 P-51’s hit shipping T/Os at Swatow and Amoy. About 70 ftrs spt Chinese ground forces by pounding
positions in Lungling area. 4 B-25’s and 12 P-40’s bomb railroad bridge at Pengpu.
