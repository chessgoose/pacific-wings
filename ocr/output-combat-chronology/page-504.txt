9/28/44 Eighth AF

Nearly 1,000 HBs attack 2 synthetic oil plants, a motor plant, and city area—all in or near Magdeburg,
Kassel, and Merseburg plus T/Os in C Germany including Eschwege A/F. 15 supporting ftr gps claim
26 aircraft destroyed. Over 30 HBs fail to return. Nearly 200 B-24’s carry fuel to France.

9/28/44 Ninth AF

9th Bomb Div hits defended area of Foret de Parroy. Ftrs escort bmrs, attack railroads W of the Rhine,
fly sweeps and armed rcn in Arnhem (from which British airborne troops have relinquished their hold

US First and Third Armies in E France and W Germany. Night patrols continue over Belgium,
Luxembourg, and Germany.
