2/8/44 Eighth AF
237 HBs attack Frankfurt/Main (using blindbombing techniques), and 127 bomb V-weapon installa-

HQ Ninth AF extends IX BC’s choice of tgts considerably, although first priority for POINTBLANK mis-

B-25’s bomb Cisterna di Latina. B-26’s hit Siena M/Y and warehouses, and railway bridge NE of Civita

and Orte, and town of Veroli, and fly strafing and bombing sweeps in Anzio battle area against motor

Roccasecca, Castello, Caprile, Piedimonte, Aquino, and other tgts, including supply dump and gun
positions. P-47’s bomb Atina. DAF Mrs (US and British) destroy large number of trucks near Sora.
