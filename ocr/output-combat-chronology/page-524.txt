Weather prevents all cmds, including 9th Bomb Div, from flight operations.

10/23/44 Twelfth AF

Bad weather grounds MBs. Ftrs and FBs although cancelling many operations, cause much damage
to transportation, destroying or damaging numerous locomotives, railroad cars, and motor transport,
and damaging vessels in Savona, Turin, Padua, and Genoa areas.

depot, and comm tgts in N Italy including M/Y at Bressanone, bridges at or near Casarsa della Delizia,
