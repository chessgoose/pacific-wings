5/3/45 Tenth AF

With the fall of Rangoon on this date (Indian 26th Div elements occupy the city), the war against the
Japanese in Burma is successfully concluded. Pockets of resistance remain W of the Irrawaddy and
between that river and the Mandalay-Pegu railway. However, during May AAF operations are reduced
drastically due to lack of suitable air tgts and because of the onset of bad weather preceding the mon-
soon. Tenth AF is withdrawn from combat and moved back to India (see 15 May 45). 1 sq of P-38’s
remains in Burma to patrol the roads leading into China.
