B-24’s bomb shipping in Bengasi harbor. B-17’s and B-24’s dispatched to attack Tobruk and Bengasi

10/16/42 Fifth AF

A-20’s and B-25’s hit village of Pawaia, trails in Kokoda area, Buna-Kokoda trail, and villages at mouth
and along Mambare R; B-25’s also bomb Mubo-Salamaua trail. B-17’s bomb A/F at Rabaul and attack
tgts in the Solomons, including A/F at Buin, and shipping off Moila Pt and Shortland Is.

10/16/42 SOPAC

Seabees on Guadalcanal patch up Henderson Field runways enough for use by ftrs. Marine ftrs and
AAF P-400’s and P-39’s begin pounding invasion force landing at Kokumbona-Doma Reef area just 8
mi away. However, the enemy continues to bombard Henderson by air strike and arty and to land
troops.

10/16/42 Eleventh AF
1 B-17, 6 B-26’s, and 4 P-38’s bomb Kiska and low-level bomb and sink 2 destroyers just N of there.
Duds hit large freighter beached off Trout Lagoon. 1 B-26 is hot down.

10/17/42 Eleventh AF
5 B-24’s bomb Kiska Main Camp area and beached vessel in Trout Lagoon. Results unobserved due
to clouds. 1 B-24 flying weather rcn finds no trace of 2 destroyers, confirming their sinking on 16 Oct.
