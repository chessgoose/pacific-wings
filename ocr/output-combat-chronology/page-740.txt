8/31/45 Twelfth AF
Twelfth AF is inactivated in Italy.

8/31/45 Fifteenth AF
Col Elmer J Rogers Jr assumes cmd of Fifteenth AF until its inactivation on 15 Sep 45.

9/2/45 International
Hostilities with Japan end officially with the signing of the instrument of surrender aboard the USS
Missouri in Tokyo Bay.

9/3/45 Eleventh AF
A C-54 on its way from Atsugi to Washington DC refuels at Adak. The aircraft carries motion pictures
of the Japanese surrender aboard the Missouri.
