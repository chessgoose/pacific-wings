8/28/44 Ninth AF

B-26’s and A-20’s of IX BC bomb fuel dumps at Doullens, Barisis-aux-Bois, ammo dump at Querrieu,
ammo and fuel dump at Compiegne/Foret de Laigue, and alcohol distillery and fuel storage depot at
Hamm. Ftrs escort BC and about 400 transports on supply and evacuation runs, attack A/Fs at
Bourges and Peronne, spt ground forces, and fly armed rcn from Amiens to E of Dijon.

8/28/44 Twelfth AF

B-25’s bomb railroad bridges in Lyon area. B-26’s destroy several airplanes at Villafranca di Verona
A/F and bridge at Parma. A-20’s hit motor transport and other T/Os during 27/28 Aug, fly armed rcn
over Po Valley and blast cmd post SE of Genoa. FBs hit vehicles in Rhone Valley, bomb and strafe
roads and bridges in battle area N of Arno R and hit shipping in Imperia and Savona harbors.

8/28/44 Fifteenth AF

560-plus HBs escorted by P-38’s and P-51’s strike Moosbierbaum oil refinery and adjacent chemical
works, oil refinery at Szony, railroad bridges and viaducts at Szolnok, Zambana, Avisio, and Ora, and
M/Ys at Miskolc.

8/28/44 Tenth AF
4 P-47’s bomb tank pool at Momauk, while 5 others hit Myintha. 2 P-47’s spt ground forces in Pinbaw
area.

8/28/44 Fourteenth AF

8 B-25’s bomb Tien Ho, White Cloud, Hankow, and Pailochi A/Fs. 8 more attack river and road traffic
from Chiuchiang to Hankow and from Hengyang to Puchi. 32 P-40’s pound T/Os at Hengyang and
Pailochi. 23 P-40’s attack Taying storage buildings and 10 P-40’s and P-51’s hit Anjen and nearby
T/Os.

8/28/44 FEAF

B-24’s hit Koror, A/F on Peleliu, and seaplane base on Arakabesan. FBs hit Kokas, vessels off Point
Karakra, barracks at Nabire, storage facilities at Moemi and Manokwari and Boram fuel dumps. A-20’s
and FBs attack small vessels along E coast of Ceram.

8/28/44 Twentieth AF
Gen Hansell assumes cmd of XXI BC. Gen Norstad succeeds Hansell as CoS of Twentieth AF.

8/28/44 Seventh AF
Saipan-based B-24’s pound Iwo Jima by day and night. P-47’s hit Pagan and Maug Is. A B-24 on
armed rcn bombs Yap. Gilberts-based B-25’s strike Ponape; Marshalls-based B-24’s hit Truk.

8/29/44 Eighth AF
5 FB gps attack rail transportation in N France, Belgium, and W Germany. 2 A/Fs, 3 M/Ys, rail lines at
several points, and a large number of rail and road vehicles are attacked.

8/29/44 Ninth AF
Bad weather allows only minimum bmr and ftr operations. B-26’s attack 1 fuel dump while a few ftrs fly
sweeps over NW France. Over 100 transports complete supply and evacuation missions.
