11/28/43 International

Tehran Conference begins and lasts until 30 Nov. Roosevelt, Churchill, and Stalin discuss further
action against Germany. OVERLORD and ANVIL are given priority over all other operations. Stalin
agrees to commit Soviet forces against Japan after Germany’s defeat.
