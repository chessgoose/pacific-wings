8/16/44 Eighth AF
Over 950 HBs attack 11 oil refineries, aircraft plants and several T/Os in C Germany. 16 ftr gps fly
over 600 spt sorties. 24 HBs are lost. Ftrs claim 32 air victories.

8/16/44 Ninth AF

The 4 gps of 98th Combat Bomb Wg move during 16-30 Aug from UK to the Continent. About 130
MBs and LBs hit Foret de Roumare ammo dump and rail bridges at Pont-Audemer, Thibouville,
Brionne, Nassandres, and Le Bourg. Ftrs escort IX BC, give air cover to armd div and inf forces, and
fly patrol and armed rcn over N and W France.

8/16/44 Twelfth AF

In spt of DRAGOON forces, firs and FBs continue to blast enemy defenses and comm on the beach-
es and in the invasion area of S France. A-20’s hit lights and vehicles during 15/16 Aug from N of
beachhead to Rhone R and during the day raid ammo stores. MBs pound Rhone R bridges and gun
positions throughout the general area.

8/16/44 Fifteenth AF

89 B-24’s, with ftr escort, bomb chemical works at Friedrichshafen. 108 B-17’s, supporting DRA-
GOON, attack railroad bridges at Saint-Vallier, Saint-Pierre-d’Albigny, Grenoble, and Isere-Valence. 42
P-51’s escort MATAF C-47’s on supply dropping mission to DRAGOON beachheads.

8/16/44 Tenth AF

As last Japanese resistance in India ceases, 16 B-25’s bomb Indaw. 20 P-51’s hit A/Fs at Lashio,
Nawnghkio, Shwebo, Anisakan, and Onbauk. 37 P-40’s and P-51’s hit strong points, pillboxes, and
machinegun positions in Pinbaw area. 19 P-47’s and P-51’s attack T/Os at Katha, bridge S of Bhamo,
town of Tagwin, railroad T/Os between Naba and Hopin, enemy-held monastery, and ammo dump at
Pegu.

8/16/44 Fourteenth AF

18 B-25’s bomb Wanling area and warehouses while 12 hit Chaling area and warehouses at Siangtan.
90 P-40’s and P-51’s hit bridges, fortified positions, troops, trucks, rivercraft, supplies, gun positions,
and other T/Os in Hsenwi and Lungling areas and in widespread area around Tungting Lake and C
Yangize R.

8/16/44 FEAF

B-24’s and B-25’s bomb Miti and Lolobata A/Fs, warehouses and shipping facilities at Ternate |, ship-
ping in Wasile and Weda Bays, installations on Ajoe and Japanese positions at Tanimbar Is. Off Alor |
B-25’s sink small vessel. A-20’s spt ground forces in Maffln Bay area while P-39’s hit troops and posi
tions from Manokwari to Windissi and bomb A/F at Ransiki, Moemi, and Waren.

8/16/44 Seventh AF
B-24’s from Saipan hit Chichi Jima and Pagan. Marshall based B-24’s bomb Truk.

8/17/44 Eighth AF

10 B-24’s dispatched against Les Foulons rail bridge abort because of cloud cover over tgt. 20 ftr gps
strafe and bomb between Brussels and Paris, 11 M/Ys, 2 A/Fs and numerous T/Os and destroy many
locomotives, train cars, and road vehicles along with bridges, towers, warehouses and barges.
