Ninth AF MBs while another gp strafes tgts in Meiningen, Coburg, and Nurnberg areas.

2/21/45 Ninth AF

9th Bomb Div hits rail overpass and bridges, oil storage depot, M/Ys, comm centers, and 13 T/Os in

railroads, A/F, and other selected tgts, fly armed rcn over wide areas of Germany including US First
Army area between Duren and Losheim, and spt VIII, XII, and XX Corps along Prum R, the West wall,
and in Saar-Mosel triangle.

2/21/45 Twelfth AF

comm to the N of the battleline and in the Po Valley.
