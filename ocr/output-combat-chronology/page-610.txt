1/31/45 Eighth AF
400-plus HBs and 4 gps of escorting ftrs are recalled from mission against tgts in Germany because
of expected bad weather at UK bases and heavy clouds.

1/31/45 Ninth AF
All operations cancelled due to weather.

1/31/45 Twelfth AF

MBs attack railroad bridges at Chiusaforte, Voghera, Lavis, Rovereto, and San Michele all’ Adige, and
M/Y at Rovereto, with only fair results. Ftrs and FBs hit bridges, rail lines, trains, vehicles, and other
tgts over widespread areas of N Italy. During 30/31 Jan LBs hit several railroad tgts during intruder
missions in Po Valley.

1/31/45 Fifteenth AF

670-plus B-24’s and B-17’s bomb Moosbierbaum oil refinery, M/Ys at Graz and Maribor, and scattered
T/Os. P-38’s and P-51’s fly over 300 escorting sorties for the HBs. Other P-38’s fly rcn and rcn escort
and drop supplies into Austria. During 30/31 Jan B-24’s drop supplies in N Italy.

1/31/45 Tenth AF

12 B-25’s hit troops and stores at Namlan. 50-plus P-47’s attack troops and supplies at several loca-
tions including Na-mawtawng, Tawkut, Mong Long, Hsipaw, Hsenwi, and Namhpakka. Transports
complete 535 sorties to forward bases and frontline areas.

1/31/45 Fourteenth AF
P-51’s strafe T/Os along Pinghan railroad.

1/31/45 Thirteenth AF
Gen Barnes again assumes cmd of XIII FC.

1/31/45 Seventh AF
20 B-24’s from Guam bomb A/Fs and AA defenses on Iwo Jima. During 31 Jan/1 Feb, 9 more HBs,
flying single-omr harassment strikes, hit the island.

2/1/45 Eighth AF

600-plus HBs hit 3 M/Ys at Mannheim, Ludwigshafen, and Krefeld, and 3 bridges at Wesel and
Mannheim, along with towns of Pforzheim and Barth and T/Os in the area. 6 ftr gps fly close and free-
lance spt.

2/1/45 Ninth AF

146 B-26’s, A-26’s, and A-20’s bomb rail bridges and defended areas on Rhine and Mosel Rivers and
in W Germany near battlefront. IX and XIX TACs escort MBs and LBs, fly armed rcn, and attack
Euskirchen M/Y and town of Arloff.

2/1/45 Twelfth AF
Bad weather curtails operations. LBs and MBs are grounded and ftrs and FBs fly only 14 sorties, 12
against comm tgts N of battle area and 2 weather rcn sorties.

2/1/45 Fifteenth AF

Over 300 B-17’s and B-24’s attack Moosbierbaum oil refinery and M/Ys at Graz, Furstenfeld, and
Klagenfurt. 1 HB bombs Pula. P-38’s and P-51’s fly 270-plus sorties escorting HBs. Other P-38’s carry
out rcn and rcn escort missions.
