12/30/43 Seventh AF

17 B-24’s, flying from Tarawa, bomb Kwajaleill. 9 B-25’s from Tarawa hit town of Jabor on Jaluit Atoll.
A-24’s from Makin, escorted by 24 P-39’s, divebomb gun positions on Mille. Adv HQ, Seventh AF, is
moved from Funafuti to Tarawa, where it remains until completion of main campaign in Gilbert and
Marshall Is.
