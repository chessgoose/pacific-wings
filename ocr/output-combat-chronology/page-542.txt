11/12/44 Seventh AF

29 Saipan-based B-24’s with P-38 escort bomb A/F on Iwo Jima. 11 P-47’s strafe runway and storage
area on Pagan. During the night a B-24 on snooper mission bombs Iwo Jima. B-24’s from Angaur
bomb Koror.

11/12/44 Eleventh AF
2 B-24’s fly armed rcn over Onnekotan and Matsuwa.

11/13/44 Ninth AF
Weather prevents all operations except for night patrol and

weather rcn by IX TAC.

11/13/44 Twelfth AF

During 12/13 Nov A-20’s bomb ammo dumps, pontoon bridge, and T/Os in Po Valley. Thick overcast
covering N Italy the following morning vastly curtails MBs which attack only a railway bridge at Padua.
FBs continue to hit comm N of battle area and also oil pipeline across the Po R at Ostiglia.

11/13/44 Fifteenth AF

During 12/13 Nov 14 MBs hit Blechhammer oil refinery. 5 others hit alternate tgts and T/Os at scat-
tered locations including Gleiwitz, Karvina, Ruzomberok, and Vac. Day operations are limited to rcn
missions.

11/13/44 Tenth AF

100-plus FBs pound numerous tgts in N Burma. Close spt strikes are made on Pinwe area, bridges at
Namhkai, Meza, and Thegyaung, on troop concentrations and on supplies at Loi-Lum and
Namhpakka. Ferry crossing at Shweli is hit by 12-hr delay bombs, Nawnghkio landing ground is
strafed, and numerous T/Os along the Irrawaddy and rail lines in N Burma are hit. Transports fly over
300 sorties to forward areas.

11/13/44 Fourteenth AF

4 B-25’s bomb Man Pwe while 4 others blast 3 warehouses at Wanling. 60-plus P-40’s, P-51’s, and P-
38’s on armed rcn over S China and as far W and SW as Burma and Thailand hit numerous T/Os
including shipping, troops, and railroad tgts.

11/13/44 FEAF

B-24’s pound Fabrica A/F while FBs hit shipping and other T/Os. A few FBs hit Legaspi A/F. B-25’s,
with P-38 cover, hit San Roque A/F and town of Zamboanga. Over Halmahera | and in Ceram area
FBs and A-20’s bomb A/Fs and various T/Os. Pegun | is blasted by 2 waves of 70 A-20’s.

11/13/44 Seventh AF

6 B-24’s from Guam, escorting Navy photo aircraft over lwo Jima and the Bonins, attack shipping at
Futamiko. 1 B-24 from Saipan, carrying out unsuccessful shipping search, bombs Iwo Jima. 7 B-24’s
from Angaur hit oil storage on Malakal | and attack bridge between Malakal and Koror.

Bad weather restricts operations to FB attacks by 17 P-47’s on rail lines and road N of battleline.
