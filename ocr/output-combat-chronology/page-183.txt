7/13/43 NAAF

During 12/13 Jul, NASAF Wellingtons hit Caltanissetta, Gerbini A/F, and Enna. During day, B-17’s, B-
25's, B-26’s, and firs attack Enna, Milo A/F, Carcitella landing ground, Randazzo, and T/Os in Sicily.
