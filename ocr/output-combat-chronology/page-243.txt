11/6/43 Seventh AF

Advanced HQ, Seventh AF, is set up on Funafuti to provide a HQ closer to tgts in the Gilbert and
Marshall Is. VII AFSC and VII BC also establish forward echelons at Funafuti. Landing fields are being
built at Baker, Nukufetau, and Nanumea to be used, along with existing fields at Canton | and
Funafuti, as operational bases for attacks on Tarawa, Makin, Mille, Maloelap, Jaluit, and Nauru. These
