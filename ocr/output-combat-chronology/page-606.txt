1/26/45 Tenth AF

8 FBs spt ground forces in Namhkam area. 140-plus FBs hit troops, supplies, guns, railroad tgts, and
T/Os at Tangtong, Panghai, Man Pwe, Kyaunghkam, Mong Pa, Nalang, Hsenwi, Molo, Mong Tat,
Humon, Tonghkan, Pangnim, Konghsa, Namtu, Namhsan, and in Nampok area. Transports fly more
than 500 sorties to advanced bases and frontline areas.

1/26/45 Fourteenth AF
15 P-51’s and P-40’s hit Chenghsien A/F and locomotives, tracks, and motor transport at Nanking and
Sinsiang.

1/26/45 FEAF

B-24’s hit coastal guns on Corregidor and in Balete Pass. B-25’s bomb Cabcaben A/F area and
coastal guns on Carabao | and A-20’s attack Grande | coastal defenses. B-26’s bomb Likanan and
Sasa. P-38’s strafe same tgts and also hit Padada. A-20’s and FBs, along with a few B-24’s, continue
armed rcn and miscellaneous attacks against A/Fs, comm, vehicles, coastal guns, town areas, and
other tgts throughout Luzon and in C Phils.

1/26/45 Twentieth AF
During 25/26 Jan, 70-plus B-29’s of XX BC mine the 6 approaches to Singapore harbor and at
Saigon, Camranh Bay, Pakchan R, Penang harbor, Koh Si Chang Channel, and Phan Rang Bay.

1/26/45 Seventh AF
17 Guam-based B-24’s bomb Iwo Jima A/Fs. During 26/27 Jan the A/Fs are hit by 9 B-24’s on individ-
ual harassment missions.

1/27/45 Ninth AF
Weather grounds 9th Bomb Div and XIX TAC. IX and XXIX TACs fly armed rcn over battle area and
parts of W Germany, along with alert flights and leaflet mission.

1/27/45 Twelfth AF

Bad weather during morning causes all MBs to abort except for an attack on bridge at Bressana
Bottarone. XXII TAC ftrs and FBs continue interdiction of comm with good results against motor trans-
port, trains, rail lines, bridges, and storage dumps. P-47’s of 57th Ftr Gp destroy oil plant near
Fornovo di Taro.

1/27/45 Fifteenth AF
For sixth consecutive day bad weather restricts operations to rcn and escort missions.

1/27/45 Tenth AF

30-plus FBs spt ground forces at Man Sak, Molo Ywama, area S of Molo, and area S of Banwe. 8 oth-
ers knock out bypass bridge at Bawgyo. About 100 FBs hit troop concentrations, supplies, and T/Os at
or near Man Kyan, Kuinkuiloi, Hohkun, Pongalau, Hsenwi, Kutkai, Hsatong, Ping hoi, Ho-mong,
Padon, and Kyaunghen. Transports fly 527 sorties to advanced bases and over forward areas, landing
men and landing and dropping supplies.

1/27/45 Fourteenth AF
22 P-40’s and P-51’s attack locomotives, trucks, and shipping at Sinsiang, Kihsien, and Nanking, from
Taiyuan to Puchou, and E of Yiyang.
