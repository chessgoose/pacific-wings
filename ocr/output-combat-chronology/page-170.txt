6/16/43 Thirteenth AF

Gen Jamison becomes CG XIII BC. Search aircraft report nearly 250 Japanese airplanes at Rabaul
and other A/Fs jammed with aircraft. In air action in the Solomons, about 120 Japanese aircraft con-
verging on Allied vessels off Tulagi and Guadalcanal are met by more than 100 Allied ftrs (Thirteenth
AF, RNZAF, USN, and USMC). The skies over Savo |, Tulagi, Cape Esperance, and Koli Pt are filled
with dogfights and flak from ship and ground guns. The battle results in the largest single-day Allied
aerial victory of the Solomons campaign. 79 airplanes are claimed shot down by Allied ftrs, and AA
claims 17 more. 6 Allied ftrs are lost. The Japanese succeed in damaging 3 ships (2 of which have to
be beached) and cause considerable destruction on Guadalcanal.
