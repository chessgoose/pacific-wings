9/4/44 Ninth AF

Weather prevents bmr activity. Ftrs fly armed rcn over Belgium, E France, Luxembourg, and E and C

and clear most resistance from Antwerp.

B-25’s and B-26’s hit several road and railroad bridges and tunnel in Po Valley while FBs strike pon-

armed rcn and offensive patrols in Po and Rhone Valleys.
