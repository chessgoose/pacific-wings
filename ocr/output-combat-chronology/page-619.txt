2/10/45 Twentieth AF
84 B-29’s, from the Marianas, hit Nakajima aircraft plant at Ota. 12 B-29’s are lost. 21 aircraft are
downed.

2/10/45 Seventh AF

10 Guam-based B-24’s bomb Okimura. 6 P-38’s escort photo mission and fly ftr sweep over Iwo Jima.
17 B-24’s from Guam, with P-38 escort, pound Iwo Jima in late afternoon, and 9 more make singlebmr
harassment strikes on the island during 10/11 Feb.

2/11/45 International
US, Britain, and USSR issue Yalta declaration.

2/11/45 Eighth AF

Over 120 B-17’s bomb Dulmen oil depot and a T/O (road junction). A P-51 gp provides close escort. 4
P-51 gps strafe rail and road transportation in E Netherlands and NW Germany, while 1 other gp
bombs and strafes a truck park in Germany.

tgts and T/Os, and attack in cooperation with US XII Corps across Sauer R NW of Echternach.

2/11/45 Fifteenth AF
Bad weather limits operations to B-24 supply mission to Yugoslavia during 10/11 Feb and to P-38 rcn

2/11/45 Tenth AF

11 B-25’s bomb troops and supplies at Loilem. 22 P-47’s spt ground forces in Mongmit area, hitting
buildings at Nabu and Japanese positions S of Myitson. 100-plus FBs pound troop concentrations,
supplies, ammo dumps, gun positions, and T/Os at several locations including Pangsang, Taung-
gaing, Panghsapye, Nam Pok, Byaungbyan, Manai, Kutkai, Mongmit, Namun, and Pangmahe-leng.
Transports complete 503 sorties to forward bases and over frontline areas (dropping supplies).

2/11/45 Fourteenth AF

17 B-25’s hit railroad yards at Sinyang and Lohochai and locomotive foundry at Hsuchang. 23 P-47’s
pound Hankow A/F. 8 ftrs hit Anyang A/F and Pinghan railroad T/Os. B-24’s claim 2 cargo vessels
sunk in S China Sea.
