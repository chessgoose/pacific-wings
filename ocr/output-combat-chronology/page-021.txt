5/31/42 Fifth AF

5/31/42 Seventh AF
B-17’s on DS at Midway begin search operations.

6/1/42 Tenth AF

6/1/42 Fifth AF
B-17’s attack Lae, Salamaua, and Rabaul.

6/1/42 Zl
Fear of attack on W coast causes suspension of movement of aircraft and crews from US to UK.

6/2/42 Fifth AF
B-17’s bomb dock and military camp area at Rabaul.

6/2/42 Seventh AF
6 of 16 B-17’s that have been on DS at Midway return to Oahu.

6/2/42 Zl
Aircraft and crews of 97th Bomb Gp, then in New England for movement to UK, are ordered to W

6/3/42 Tenth AF

A flight of 6 B-25’s of 11th Bomb Sq earmarked for CATF, take off from Dinjan for China. They bomb
Lashio en route to Kunming, but afterward 3 crash into an overcast-hidden mountain at 10,000 ft and
another is abandoned when it runs out of fuel near Chan-i. The remaining 2 B-25’s reach Kunming, 1
with its radio operator killed by a ftr.

6/3/42 Fifth AF
B-17’s hit wharf, warehouse area, and military camp at Rabaul.

6/3/42 Seventh AF

Preliminary action begins in Battle of Midway. 9 B-17’s, flying out of Midway, attack 5 large warships
570 mi off Midway, claiming 5 hits and several near misses. 7 other B-17’s leave Oahu and fly to
Midway.
