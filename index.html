    About Pacific Wings

    A historical flight tracker for US Army Air Forces operations in the Pacific Theater, 1941–1945. Missions are visualized in real time along the timeline, with routes derived from operational records.

    Sources

    • USAF Historical Division, Air Force Historical Research Agency (AFHRA) — mission chronologies and operational summaries
    • The B-29 Superfortress — aircraft records and serial numbers
    • Historical basemap: aourednik/historical-basemaps (1938 GeoJSON)
